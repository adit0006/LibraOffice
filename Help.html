Need Help?



The LibraOffice Community is inclusive - everyone is welcome, whether you're an end user or want to become more involved. LibraOffice is created and maintained by its community. There are many ways you can find and access community assistance.


Contact

Twitter

You can contact the community through our various Twitter pages:


  • Our official Twitter page: LibraOffice

  • Our Q&A Twitter page, for those who need answer on various things: Ask.LibraOffice.org

  • Our bug fixing and bug report Twitter page. A place to report on bugs that need to be fixed : LibraOffice Bugzilla

  • We also have a blog, where we post update and news on the newest things happening on the LibraOffice Community: LibraOffice Blog
