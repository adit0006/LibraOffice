Get Involved

Welcome! LibraOffice is developed by a friendly community, made up of hundreds of contributors around the world. It's free and open source software, so anyone can study how it works and add improvements. Join us today and help us to make it even better! Check out the quick tasks below.


Quick tasks

Got a minute?

Got a spare ten or twenty minutes, and want to give us a hand? Check our these quick tasks:

  • Upvote well-written questions on Ask LibraOffice (after getting 5 karma points)
  • Browse our Frequently Asked Questions pages, and check they're up-to-date (or translate them)
  • Update or improve our marketing materials and flyers
  • Check and confirm new bug reports to help our QA team
